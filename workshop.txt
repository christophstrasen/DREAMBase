title=[42SP] DREAMBase - base library mod for the DREAM suite of mods
description=[h1]DREAMBase[/h1]
description=
description=[i]A small “base library” mod for the [url=https://github.com/christophstrasen/DREAM]DREAM[/url] family of mods (Build 42).[/i]
description=
description=[h2]Scope[/h2]
description=It aims to provide shared utilities that work both:
description=[list]
description=[*]in [b]Project Zomboid B42[/b] (Lua 5.1 / Kahlua), and
description=[*]in [b]headless vanilla Lua 5.1[/b] (busted/tests, CLI scripts).
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]Module overview[/h2]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/log")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Tagged, leveled logger.
description=[*]Delegates to `require("LQR/util/log")` when available; otherwise uses a compatible fallback logger.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/util")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Small pure helpers (assert/log helpers, deterministic key/hash helpers, simple cache, selection helpers).
description=[*]Includes `subscribeEvent(...)` for adapting PZ `Events.*` and Starlit `LuaEvent` sources.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/time_ms")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Best-effort `gameMillis()` and `cpuMillis()` clock helpers (ms).
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/events")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Event interop helpers (currently a thin wrapper around `DREAMBase/util.subscribeEvent`).
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/pz/java_list")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Defensive helpers for Java-backed lists/arrays in Kahlua (`size`, `get`).
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/pz/safe_call")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Safe engine method invocation helper (`safeCall(obj, methodName, ...)`).
description=[/list]
description=
description=[b]`require("DREAMBase/bootstrap")`[/b]
description=[list]
description=[*]Headless-only loader convenience (best-effort `package.path` extension); should be a no-op in PZ.
description=[/list]
description=
description=[h2]In-game usage[/h2]
description=Add `\DREAMBase` to your `mod.info` `require=` list, then:
description=
description=[code]
description=local U = require("DREAMBase/util")
description=local Log = require("DREAMBase/log").withTag("MY.MOD")
description=[/code]
